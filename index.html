About Us

Hayas foundation was founded with a selfless spirit to make a contribution to the world, by empowering the female gender, providing the necessary skills that will advance their lifestyles as women. We exist to create a web of passionate women willing to come together to help lift other women

Goals

In 5 years (By 2023)

Take these females through the journey of the particular business world; *Visit stores and brands in the particular entrepreneurial skills. *Teach them how to grow that kind of business and make money. *Pair them with possible mentors in the respective fields.

In 5 years (By 2023)

Train 60 females in 4 different entrepreneurship skills(i.e 15 each) Skills -> Cloth stoning(fashion), cake baking etc.

In 5 years (By 2023)

Teach them to use Social media to their advantage; Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter etc.

In 5 years (By 2023)

To have females who have become experts in their own fields teach and mentor the younger generation females, which would continue to increase the number of impacts we make.

In 5 years (By 2023)

Build a large community where top companies and businesses seek to hire our very skilled females.
